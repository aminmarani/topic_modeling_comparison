This prototype presents posts social media users were publicly posting about Eating Disorder. If a posts is really long, the prototype only shows the beginning. For those ones, to see the whole post on Tumblr or Instagram, you can click on the “Read the entire Post” Link.

Think about a moment in the past and choose one post to explore that you experienced during ED recovery or you are interested in exploring further.

You can explore posts that users posted after the orignal posts by clicking on "What others posted next" and see the proportion by clicking on "Show Proportions".
